Banana Cake Recipe

Banana Cake

This is an easy and convenient banana cake recipe that only requires a few ingredients you'll already have in your pantry!

Our moist and fluffy banana cake recipe will definitely have your guests coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 3/4 cups of caster sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 125g of butter
  • 1 1/2 cups of self-raising flour
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla essence

Steps:

  1. Melt butter, sugar and vanilla essence in a saucepan over medium low heat
  2. Remove from heat
  3. Add mashed bananas and stir through until combined
  4. Add egg and mix in well
  5. Stir in flour
  6. Slowly pour in milk and make sure mixture is well combined
  7. Transfer to baking tin and bake for 40 minutes at 170 degrees celcius
