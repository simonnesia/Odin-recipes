Chocolate Cake Recipe

Chocolate Cake

This is a rich and decadent no fuss chocolate cake that only takes a few minutes to whip up.

As easy as store bought mix but twice the deliciousness!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla essence
  • 1 cup of boiling water

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees celcius
  2. In a large bowl, mix together all the dry ingredients (sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, salt) until thoroughly combined
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla essence and mix on medium speed of your mixer for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in boiling water; mixture will be a bit watery but do not worry!
  5. Pour evenly into greased pan and bake for 30 - 35 minutes in preheated oven.
  6. Check with a toothpick to make sure batter is cooked thoroughly, cool for 10 minutes then serve!
