Simple Sponge Cake Recipe

Sponge Cake

Our simple sponge cake recipe can be made to be enjoyed as is, or as the basis for delectable layered cakes.

Soft, spongy and delicious, this is a perfect cake for beginners and seasoned bakers alike!

Ingredients

  • 1 cups of caster sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of hot water
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla essence

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees celcius
  2. Whip eggs in mixer on high speed until foamy (approx 2 minutes)
  3. Gradually add sugar 1 tablespoon at a time, while continuing to whip. Whip for approx 20 minutes until the mixture forms a thick ribbon when falling off the whisk.
  4. Add flour and baking powder into whipped eggs and carefully fold into the mixture. Do not overwork the ingredients as the cake will be dense and doughy.
  5. In a cup, mix together melted butter, hot water and vanilla extract. Mix into batter until thoroughly incorporated.
  6. Pour evenly into greased pan and bake for 30 - 35 minutes in preheated oven.
  7. Check with a toothpick to make sure batter is cooked thoroughly, then serve!
